Welcome to the Biggest
Internet of Things Showcase

The IoT Grand Expo'23 Edition-1 is an attempt of showcasing the industrial potential of our passionated and hardworking students of the Electronics and Communication Engineering Department at Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies, Nuzvid, India. Our event features keynote speeches and project demonstrations from experts in the field, offering a unique opportunity to learn and experience the latest technology. Witness how IoT is transforming industries and changing the way we live and work. Join us at IGE'23, the premier event that brings technology and design together to showcase cutting-edge projects that are shaping the future. Let us inspire you with the latest advancements.
